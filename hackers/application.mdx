---

The DeltaHacks application allows you to apply for the hackathon. Your application is automatically saved as you fill it out, so you can return at any time before submitting.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with OAuth">
    Navigate to the application page and sign in using OAuth authentication. Once authenticated, you'll be redirected to the application form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete the application form">
    Fill out all required fields. Your progress is automatically saved to local storage.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit your application">
    Review your information and click Submit. Once submitted, you'll be redirected to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Application Form Sections

### Personal Information

All fields in this section are **required** unless marked optional.

- **First Name** - Your legal first name
- **Last Name** - Your legal last name  
- **Phone Number** - Contact phone number
- **Country of Residence** - Select from dropdown (Canada listed first)
- **Birthday** - Must be at least 18 years old
- **Resume** (Optional) - Upload PDF file (max 5 MB)

### Education

- **Currently enrolled in post-secondary education?** - Checkbox

If enrolled, the following fields appear (all optional):
- **Study Location** - University/institution name
- **Study Degree** - Type of degree program
- **Study Major** - Field of study
- **Year of Study** - Current year in program
- **Expected Graduation** - Anticipated graduation date
- **Previous Hackathons Count** (Optional) - Number of hackathons attended

### Long Answer Questions

All long answer questions have a **150 word limit** and are required:

1. If you could instantly master any hobby, what would it be and why?
2. Why do you want to be a part of DeltaHacks 12?
3. Talk about a topic that can make you lose track of time, why does it captivate you?
4. Describe a situation where you had to quickly learn a new skill or technology to solve a problem. What was your approach and what did you learn from it?
5. If you did not have to worry about school/money/time, what is the one thing you would work on?

### Survey

- **Social Links** - Optional links to your social media profiles
- **Interests** (Optional) - Anything else interesting you want us to know
- **T-shirt Size** - Required for swag
- **What kind of hacker are you?** - Multi-select (e.g., designer, developer, hardware hacker)
- **Workshop Interests** (Optional) - Select workshops you'd like to attend
- **How did you hear about DeltaHacks?** - Multi-select
- **Already have a team?** - Checkbox
- **Coffee chat with sponsor?** - Checkbox to be considered for sponsor coffee chats

### Emergency Contact

All fields required:
- **Name** - Emergency contact name
- **Relation** - Relationship (parent/guardian/friend/spouse)
- **Phone Number** - Emergency contact phone

### MLH Survey and Consent

<Note>
These checkboxes are for MLH partnership. Your information will only be shared with MLH if the partnership is finalized.
</Note>

**Diversity Questions** (All optional):
- Do you identify as part of an underrepresented group in tech?
- Gender
- Sexual orientation  
- Ethnic background

**Required MLH Agreements:**
- **MLH Code of Conduct** - Required
- **MLH Privacy Policy** - Required to share application info with MLH
- **MLH Communications** (Optional) - Receive emails about events and opportunities

## Application Status Tracking

After submitting, your application will go through several status stages visible on your dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="IN_REVIEW">
    Your application has been received and is being reviewed by the DeltaHacks team.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="ACCEPTED">
    Congratulations! You've been invited to attend DeltaHacks 12. You must RSVP to confirm attendance.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="WAITLISTED">
    You've been placed on the waitlist. You'll be notified if a spot becomes available.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="REJECTED">
    Unfortunately, we were unable to offer you a spot this year. We encourage you to apply next year!
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="RSVP">
    You've confirmed your attendance. Check your email for the attendee package and event details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="CHECKED_IN">
    You've checked in at the event. Welcome to DeltaHacks!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Auto-save Feature

Your application progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage as you type. This means:

- You can close the browser and return later
- Your draft is preserved until you submit
- Each user account has its own saved draft

<Warning>
Clearing your browser's local storage will delete your draft. Submit your application to ensure it's saved to the server.
</Warning>

## After Submission

Once submitted:
1. You'll be redirected to your dashboard
2. You cannot edit your application (contact hello@deltahacks.com if changes are needed)
3. You'll receive email updates about your application status
4. Check your dashboard regularly for status updates

## Application Deadline

Applications close when the `killApplications` config is set to true. You'll see a message indicating applications are closed and cannot submit after the deadline.
