The hacker dashboard is your central hub for managing your DeltaHacks experience. What you see depends on your application status.

## Dashboard Views by Status

Your dashboard displays different content based on your current application status:

### IN_REVIEW

After submitting your application:

- Confirmation message that your application was received
- Reminder to check email for updates
- Information about prep events on social media
- Link to FAQ page
- Contact email: hello@deltahacks.com

<Note>
While applications are open, you previously had the option to withdraw and resubmit. This option is removed once applications close.
</Note>

### ACCEPTED

When you've been accepted:

- Congratulations message with event details
- **RSVP Button** - Click to confirm attendance
- Event date: January 10-11, 2026
- FAQ link
- Contact information

#### RSVP Process

Clicking the RSVP button opens a modal where you can:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share resume with sponsors">
    Check the box if you want to share your resume with event sponsors for recruiting opportunities.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide dietary restrictions">
    Enter any dietary restrictions or allergies in the text field (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut allergy).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm RSVP">
    Click Confirm to complete your RSVP, or Cancel to close the modal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After confirming, your status changes to RSVP.

### WAITLISTED

- Message explaining waitlist status
- Encouragement to check email for updates
- FAQ link
- Contact information

### REJECTED

- Thank you message for applying
- Explanation that we couldn't accommodate all applicants
- Encouragement to apply next year
- FAQ link
- Contact information

### RSVP (Confirmed Attendance)

After RSVPing, you get access to:

**Quick Links:**
- **Check-In QR Code** - Rainbow-bordered button (primary action)
- **FAQ** - Frequently asked questions
- **Discord** - Join the community server
- **Schedule** - View event schedule
- **Attendee Package** - Important event information

<Note>
You'll receive the Attendee Package in your email with important information about registration, venue location, and event details.
</Note>

### CHECKED_IN (At Event)

Once checked in at the event:

**WiFi Information:**
- Network name and password displayed
- QR code for easy WiFi connection (desktop view in sidebar)
- Mobile view shows WiFi info in a card at the top

**Dashboard Actions:**
- **Schedule** - View full event schedule
- **My Profile** - Access your QR code and profile
- **Attendee Package** - Event information and resources

## Profile Management

From the dashboard, you can access your profile which includes:

- Your name and education details
- QR code for event check-in and meal redemption
- Apple Wallet and Google Wallet integration
- Social media links (if provided)

See [QR Codes](/hackers/qr-codes) for more details.

## Schedule Access

The Schedule page shows:
- Event timeline and activities
- Workshop times and locations  
- Meal times
- Judging presentation slots
- Other important event milestones

## Navigation Tabs

The dashboard includes navigation tabs:
- **Dashboard** - Main status and information page
- **Schedule** - Event timeline and activities

<Note>
Judges see different dashboard content and are automatically redirected to the judging interface.
</Note>
